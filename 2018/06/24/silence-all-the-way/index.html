Silence All The Way

First posted on renren.com, 2009.07.24

Never think of writing some peom-like things, I had must been very depressed at that time. Maybe it’s the only antique I left for my first unrequited love, my valuable memories, thank you all.

Tonight despair comes

Nobody to talk to

All the mess turn around

Nothing to think

Nothing to consider

Want to call but courage disappear

Miss miss miss

Maybe it’s a chance god bless

Loneliness

Familiar or strange

I’ve no idea

Life appears what it appears to be

Do it on my own

Miss it on my way

Can you feel the same way?

No, maybe not

It’s about finding root

But i couldn’t make it

All the time

On the go

It’s my feat?

Or it is to be.

Pieces of sorrow

Chewing, swallowing, hurting

Goodnight my friend

Thanks for listening to me for a long time

I’m just in the longest distant in love

It’s not a poem nor something

Messy words truely feelings

Adaptation, adjustment, here i come

Thanks Mushroom!

Tonight you light me up
